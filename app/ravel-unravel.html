RavelUnravel

ROLE: Drupal, SASS/CSS, Javascript

RavelUnravel is a project started by Project Interfaith. They're exploring the tapestry of religious and spiritual identities that make up our communities and the complexities of how we construct and deconstruct identity.

I originally teamed up with Secret Penguin in developing this site. Over the course of the last few years I've continued to help in developing new features.
